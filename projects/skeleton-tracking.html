Skeleton Tracking

This is a very interesting project as it repurposes a Kinect from an Xbox 360 to use it as a depth camera and employ it as a user interface method instead of traditional methods.


As examples of application for this sensor, I created a robot in the style of an arm with two degrees of freedom that replicated the movement of one of my arms using forward kinematics. I also developed a more complex second prototype of an intelligent arm that could reach any point in space autonomously using inverse kinematics.


In addition to my projects, I made a great contribution to the community as it seemed that all the resources on this topic had disappeared over 10 years ago. Somehow, I was able to retrieve them and share a video about the installation process and how to start programming this depth camera, completely free and in Spanish. This has been recognized by many users on the internet.
