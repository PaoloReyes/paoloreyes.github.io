What is it about?

This project originated from a task during my third semester of high school, where a group of friends and I were tasked with developing a prototype that addressed a specific problem. In collaboration with my robotics team, we came up with the idea of creating an electric wheelchair in which each wheel was powered by a motor. The speed and direction of the wheelchair were controlled by the tilting motion of an accelerometer placed in a headband worn by the user. The accelerometer data was interpreted by an Arduino Uno, making it easy for the user to navigate through space.
