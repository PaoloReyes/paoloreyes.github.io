What is it about?

Aerodesign is a student group from Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Toluca, dedicated to creating unmanned aircraft for the SAE Aerodesign competition. This project encompasses a complete engineering process, starting from the design and 3D modeling of the aircraft, including topological, material strength, and aerodynamic simulations. It extends to the actual manufacturing and electronic wiring of the aircraft.


In the team, my main role was in 3D design, where I focused on reducing the weight of the wing and providing mobility to the ailerons to ensure proper aircraft control. I also participated in the assembly of the entire plane and the manufacturing of the landing gear.
