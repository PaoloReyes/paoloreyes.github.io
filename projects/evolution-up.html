What is it about?

A non-profit organization that I founded with my partner Erick García with the purpose of seeking ways to promote science and technology. From this organization, the idea arose to sponsor robotics teams by manufacturing their parts at no cost, thus fostering technological development for teams with limited machining capabilities.


In addition to supporting these teams, we also engage in volunteer work to assist marginalized sectors of the population through donations and courses, helping them find opportunities to succeed by using science and technology as a tool.


We are currently working on creating a curriculum to open a robotics school that can participate in FIRST competitions (one of the most famous robotics competitions worldwide) and is planned to start competing in the 2024 season.
