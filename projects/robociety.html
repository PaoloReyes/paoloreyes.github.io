Robociety

Robociety is a student group from the Tecnológico de Monterrey Campus Toluca that participates in a wide variety of robotics competitions. Some of these include:

  • Sumo.
  • Mini Sumo.
  • Micro Sumo.
  • Maze.
  • Robot war.
  • Line follower.

My role in the team is as the leader of control systems. I am responsible for programming low-level libraries for precise movements using encoders. I also teach the rest of the team about programming with Arduino, directly accessing registers to use interrupts and hidden features of the microcontroller (ATmega328P) to achieve better results with our robots.


During the semester that I was part of the team, we competed in Oaxaca and Tlaxcala, achieving very good results despite most of the team members being new.
