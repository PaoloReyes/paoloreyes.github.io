Custom Bookshelf for Church

As part of one of the services I offer, which is manufacturing, my partner and I received an order to design a custom bookshelf for an unused room in a church located near our workshop.


The general idea was to select a wall and create a series of deep boxes where large books could be placed, and on the second wall, install shelves for smaller books.


For the drawers, the measurements of the wall were taken into account, and a design was chosen to organize them in a somewhat creative way while maintaining a structure of rows and columns. As for the shelves, wooden shelves were designed with a front stopper to hold the books in place and prevent them from falling.
