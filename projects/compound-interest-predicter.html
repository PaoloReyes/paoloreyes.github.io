What is it about?

This is a tool I made for a YouTube video, the intention was to serve as a visual orientation for viewers to understand the concept of compound interest that I show based on its differential equation, but in a more intuitive way so they can test their own capabilities of investing and see real predictions of what they could wait in the future.
