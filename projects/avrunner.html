What is it about?

AVRunner is a groundbreaking tool designed to transform the landscape of AVR microcontroller development by seamlessly integrating the power and flexibility of Visual Studio Code (VSCode). No more wrestling with clunky, outdated IDEs – AVRunner brings a breath of fresh air to the world of embedded systems programming.


Gone are the days of cumbersome IDEs with limited functionality and steep learning curves. AVRunner simplifies the development process by harnessing the robust capabilities of VSCode, a popular and versatile code editor trusted by developers worldwide. With AVRunner, you can wave goodbye to frustration and embrace a smoother, more intuitive coding experience.
