What is it about?

The course is the most popular content on my YouTube channel. It consists of a series of 18 videos where I teach various topics ranging from the basics of Boolean algebra to the internal functioning of a timer in a microcontroller. The course is designed for advanced users, as it utilizes the 'C' programming language as a tool. It does not aim to teach programming or provide foundational knowledge in electronics. Instead, it builds upon these foundational concepts to access the microcontroller's registers and demonstrates how modifying bits can change internal configurations and control higher-level devices, enabling complex interactions.
